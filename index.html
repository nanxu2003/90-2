Frequently Asked Questions

Which operating systems do you support?

We support all major Linux distributions (CentOS, Ubuntu, Debian, Alpine) and the full Windows Server range. Additionally, we provide operations configuration for enterprise-grade systems including FreeBSD and custom builds.

We don't have servers yet. Can you help us procure them?

Whilst we don't directly sell server hardware, we are accredited partners with AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform. We can procure cloud resources on your behalf at preferential rates, or provide comprehensive hardware procurement recommendations for on-premises setups.

How do you ensure data security?

We operate under strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs). During operations, we use bastion hosts to audit all actions, encrypt all sensitive data at rest and in transit, and never access core business data without explicit authorisation. We're also GDPR compliant.

What is your typical response time for critical incidents?

For Professional package clients, we guarantee a 30-minute response time for critical incidents, 24/7. Essential package clients receive support during business hours with 2-hour response times. Emergency ad-hoc support is available on a case-by-case basis.

Can you work with our existing IT team?

Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with in-house IT departments, providing supplementary expertise, handling complex infrastructure challenges, or offering training and knowledge transfer sessions to upskill your team.
