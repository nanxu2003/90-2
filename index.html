About HELIOS TRADE LIMITED

Where Engineering Meets Artistry

HELIOS TRADE LIMITED is a comprehensive engineering and technology services firm headquartered in the United Kingdom. We're not just designers—we're problem solvers who transform complex challenges into elegant, functional solutions.

In an era of increasingly sophisticated building requirements and digital transformation, we've moved beyond traditional service models. By integrating "engineering excellence" with "digital intelligence", we deliver end-to-end solutions—from conceptual planning through to implementation. Whether optimising commercial space circulation or architecting enterprise network infrastructure, we pursue perfection in every detail.

  • Precision engineering surveying and planning
  • Forward-thinking digital network architecture
  • Sustainable, environmentally conscious design
  • Integrated project management from concept to completion

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of engineering projects do you undertake?

We specialise in office building smart retrofits, community facility infrastructure projects, server room construction, structured cabling, and associated interior design and build projects across commercial and public sectors.

Do you provide services outside the UK?

Whilst our core operations are UK-based to ensure rapid on-site response and aftercare, we undertake international projects on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What is your project warranty period?

Standard engineering projects come with a 1-3 year warranty period, whilst network equipment follows manufacturer warranty terms. We also offer long-term managed maintenance service packages for ongoing peace of mind.

Do you handle planning permissions and building regulations?

Yes, we provide comprehensive planning consultancy services, helping navigate local authority requirements and ensuring full compliance with UK Building Regulations throughout the project lifecycle.
